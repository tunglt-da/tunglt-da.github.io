Real-Time Sales Forecast System
Situation

The business lacked a reliable revenue forecasting system. Management relied on end-of-day manual reports with no intra-day visibility, making it impossible to react to revenue shortfalls in real time or evaluate performance against KPI targets across different business dimensions.

Task

Design and build a multi-dimensional, real-time sales forecasting system that auto-refreshes every 30 minutes, provides daily snapshots at start-of-day, and enables comparison of actual revenue vs forecast vs snapshot vs KPI — sliceable by business unit, customer nationality, campaign, and product/service category.

Action
  • Built automated data pipelines to ingest transactional data and generate forecasts refreshing every 30 minutes
  • Implemented daily snapshot logic to capture start-of-day baselines for performance tracking
  • Designed multi-dimensional forecast breakdowns by business unit, customer nationality, marketing campaign, and product/service category
  • Created comparison dashboards showing Actual vs Forecast vs Snapshot vs KPI with variance highlighting and trend indicators
  • Built alerting mechanisms for significant deviations between actuals and forecast targets
Technologies
SQL BigQuery Power BI Python Airflow Google Sheets API
Result
  • Enabled real-time revenue monitoring with 30-min granularity, replacing manual end-of-day reports
  • Management gained multi-dimensional visibility to identify underperforming segments and campaigns within the same day
  • Improved forecast accuracy and KPI tracking, enabling proactive resource allocation and tactical adjustments

Onboarding Journey & Cross-sell Optimization
Situation

Cross-sell conversion was low because customer engagement after initial purchase was unstructured. There was no systematic flow to re-engage customers across communication channels, resulting in missed upsell opportunities and low repeat purchase rates.

Task

Design and implement an omni-channel onboarding journey that automatically engages customers from the moment an order is created, using the right channel at the right time to maximize cross-sell conversion.

Action
  • Mapped the full customer journey from order creation through post-purchase engagement, identifying optimal touchpoints for cross-sell messaging
  • Designed multi-channel engagement flows across App Push, EDM, WhatsApp, ZNS, and SMS with channel prioritization logic
  • Built trigger-based automation: each flow activates upon order event, with sequenced messaging and fallback channels
  • Implemented A/B testing frameworks to optimize message timing, content, and channel mix
  • Created performance dashboards tracking funnel conversion at each touchpoint and channel-level effectiveness
Technologies
SQL BigQuery CRM Platform A/B Testing Power BI Funnel Analysis
Result
  • Increased cross-sell conversion rate by establishing a structured, data-driven engagement flow
  • Identified the highest-performing channel combinations for different customer segments
  • Reduced reliance on single-channel outreach, improving overall customer reachability and engagement

OTA Website Analytics & Performance Optimization
Situation

The OTA platform generated data across multiple business and operational areas, including website traffic, customer journeys, booking funnels, sales performance, marketing campaigns, and operational processes. However, performance data was fragmented across different sources, making it difficult for Business Directors, Operations, and Digital Marketing teams to obtain a unified view of the business and quickly identify performance gaps and growth opportunities.

Task

Designed and developed a comprehensive OTA analytics solution to provide a centralized view of business performance, customer behavior, conversion funnels, marketing effectiveness, and operational health, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs and make faster, data-driven decisions.

Action
  • Designed an analytics framework covering Executive, Business, Operational, Customer, and Marketing perspectives to address different stakeholder needs.
  • Built executive-level performance monitoring covering GMV, bookings, AOV, conversion rate, cancellation rate, and booking success rate.
  • Analyzed the end-to-end customer conversion funnel from website sessions and product discovery through checkout and successful booking, identifying drop-off points and conversion opportunities.
  • Analyzed traffic and acquisition performance across Organic, Paid, Direct, Affiliate, and Referral channels, including channel-level conversion, GMV, AOV, and customer acquisition.
  • Developed campaign and marketing performance analysis to evaluate traffic, conversion, revenue, and campaign effectiveness, supporting Digital Marketing teams in optimizing channel and campaign performance.
  • Analyzed customer behavior across the website, including new vs. returning users, engagement, customer journeys, and retention patterns.
  • Monitored operational health across the booking funnel, including cart abandonment, payment drop-off, booking failures, and technical errors, helping Operations teams identify potential issues affecting customer conversion.
  • Integrated business and event-level data from multiple sources to provide a unified analytics view and establish a single source of truth for business performance monitoring.
Technologies
SQL BigQuery dbt Power BI Event Tracking Funnel Analysis E-commerce Analytics Marketing Analytics Customer Journey
Result
  • Provided Business Directors with a centralized view of OTA business performance across revenue, bookings, conversion, and operational KPIs.
  • Enabled Operations teams to identify funnel drop-offs and potential operational issues affecting booking conversion and customer experience.
  • Enabled Digital Marketing teams to evaluate channel and campaign quality based on traffic, conversion, revenue, and customer acquisition performance.
  • Established a unified analytics framework that connected business, customer, marketing, and operational data, supporting more consistent and data-driven decision-making.

MiniApp Performance Analytics
Situation

A MiniApp embedded within a super app lacked comprehensive analytics. Business, marketing, and operations teams had no unified view of performance — revenue trends, marketing funnel health, promotion effectiveness, and operational quality were tracked in scattered spreadsheets or not tracked at all.

Task

Build an end-to-end analytics framework covering all critical dimensions: business performance, marketing effectiveness, financial efficiency, and operational health.

Action

Business Overview

  • Built revenue & traffic trend dashboards with GMV, Orders, Traffic by day/week/month and DoD/MoM/QoQ/YoY growth %
  • Tracked core sales KPIs: Conversion Rate, AOV, ASP, OOS Rate, Revenue, CTR
  • Created monthly Top 100 SKU rankings by revenue and by orders

Financial Efficiency

  • Measured promotion effectiveness: GMV vs Promotion Cost, % Budget utilization, ROAS/ROI analysis

Marketing Effectiveness

  • Traffic source attribution by channel with UV and CTR tracking
  • Built full marketing funnel: Item Search → Impression → Item Click → Listing Page → Detail Page → Add to Cart → Checkout → Payment → Order Complete
  • Identified funnel drop-off points with stage-by-stage conversion analysis
  • Tracked ad efficiency: CPC, CPM, Cost per Conversion
  • Monitored Repeat Purchase Rate for retention insights

Operational Health

  • Tracked shop-level quality metrics: Rating Score, Review Score, Booking Cancellation Rate, Return Rate
Technologies
SQL BigQuery Power BI Looker Studio Python Airflow Firebase Analytics
Result
  • Delivered a single source of truth for MiniApp performance across business, marketing, finance, and operations
  • Enabled marketing team to pinpoint exact funnel drop-off stages and optimize conversion at each step
  • Promotion ROI visibility allowed finance team to optimize budget allocation and reduce inefficient spend
  • Operational metrics improved shop quality accountability with data-driven performance reviews
